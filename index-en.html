About Us

HoszMet is a local leader in the steel industry. We design, build, and assemble steel structures tailored to your needs. We use sandwich panels and trapezoidal sheets for cladding. We have been operating in the market for many years, combining experience with modern technological solutions.

Our Offer

We provide cladding for structures with sandwich panels and trapezoidal sheets, ensuring durability and aesthetics.

Technical Consulting

Our experts will help you select the optimal solutions and materials for your project.
